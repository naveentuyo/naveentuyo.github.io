Mario Kart Game

Key Features

  • Fun karting physics
  • Drifting
  • Multiplayer
  • Items
  • Controller, Mobile, and PC Compatible
  • Mini-Boosting
  • Time Trials
  • Off-roading

This is my recreation the Mario Kart racing mechanics from scratch. This was a 3-month project I made during the summer. It emulates the feel and fun of playing Mario Kart! This was created using Roblox Studio and scripted in Luau (subset of Lua). Everything has been scripted by me, including the kart physics, items, drifting and more. A 3d artist decorated the map and created the VFX.

Every force vector is calcuted per-frame, resulting in fluid controls while still being preformant. Players can play using a controller, keyboard + mouse, or mobile device. It also supports multiplayer, so players can race and challenge each other! I really enjoyed building this game, I may even go back and add some new features.
